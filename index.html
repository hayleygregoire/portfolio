About Me

I'm Hayley Gregoire, a graduate student at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism specializing in sports media. My goal is to become an on-air sideline reporter.

Currently, I work as a Basketball Communications Associate with the Chicago Bulls, where I transcribe press conferences and manage postgame audio distribution. This role has deepened my understanding of professional sports media operations and strengthened my ability to work efficiently under tight deadlines.

I'm bilingual in English and Spanish, which has helped me in connecting with diverse communities and sources. I'm extremely adaptable and would say it's one of my biggest strengths — From teaching English to elementary students in Spain to supporting production for the Tony Awards in New York, to working as a video production intern for SiriusXM, I've found that I was quickly able to adapt to a different cultural setting and perform well. I am enthuasic, have a keen attention to detail and always seek out crituqes to find ways where I can grow.

I graduated summa cum laude from Fordham University with a B.A. in Communications & Culture and Spanish, and was also a student in the Honors College.
